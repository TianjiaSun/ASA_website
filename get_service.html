Get Started to Learn

STEP.1 You need to have a look at the full list of service APPs and understand how many services you will use.
STEP.2 You may try those services at "Get Started" page.
STEP.3 Sign up as a GAUSIAN worldwide developer, and download GAUSIAN's ASA_Client_Dummy.js.
STEP.4 Use ASA_Client_Dummy.js in your project, so it provides ASA API and it always link to a "Dummy Company"
STEP.5 You are free to the "Dummy Company" to develop your user APPs.

Release your user-APP

STEP.1 Log in as a GAUSIAN worldwide developer.
STEP.2 Upload source code of your User APP.
STEP.3 Input relevant informaiton like APP name, functions, price, suportted APP-link index, et al.
STEP.4 Wait for censorship and compatibility test.
STEP.5 Your user APP is available in GAUSIAN Store.
