ASA Exchanger

ASA-Exchanger is a core of GAUSIAN
for data and service exchanging among
worldwide APPs.

Service APPs (open to developers)

Service-APPs are hosted on servers
to provide data and services like
"a unified interface to worldwide e-commerce websites",
"a unified interface for shipment and tracking",
"reading and writing existing database",
"matching better supplier",
use your imagine...

User APPs (open to developers)

User APPs run on client side.
They get data and service via ASA Exchanger.
Sometimes, User-APPs pay for services.
