Service-APP Development Flow

STEP.1 Have an idea that what service you want to provide. It'd better be creative.
STEP.2 Sign up as a GAUSIAN worldwide developer, and download GAUSIAN's ASA_Server_Dummy.php.
STEP.3 ASA_Server_Dummy.php in your project, so it provides ASA API and it always link to a "Dummy Company" database.
STEP.4 Upload your service PHP to ASA server.
STEP.5 You are free to try your service at "Get Started" page and it is in the "Testing" tab.

Service-APP Release Flow

STEP.1 Log in as a GAUSIAN worldwide developer.
STEP.2 Upload PHP source code of your service APP.
STEP.3 Input relevant informaiton like APP name, functions, API documents, User-APP example codes, price, et al.
STEP.4 Wait for censorship and compatibility test.
STEP.5 Your service APP is released.
STEP.6 You can try it in "Get Started" page and it is in the "Released" tab.
