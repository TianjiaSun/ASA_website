How to Development


Welcome to GAUSIAN. Developing User APP on GAUSIAN Desktop is simple.

STEP.1:
Please go to https://github.com/gausian-developers to clone a User APP template.
In the template,we showed a simple example of how to integrate ASA request into your User APP based on Angular.

STEP.2:
Please have a look at the full list of service APPs and think about how many services you will use for your User APP development.

STEP.4:
Read APIs of the service APPs you need to use and try to talk to them here.
STEP.5:
Integrate those talking into your user APP.
